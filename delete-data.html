Request Data Deletion

Last updated: 2025-12-05 07:10:21 UTC

If you have used our application and would like your stored data to be deleted, you may request it at any time.

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data:

  • Send an email to: stockpilot.support@gmail.com
  • Include your registered email address or account details
  • Clearly state that you want your data deleted

Processing Time

We will process your data deletion request and remove all your information within 7 business days.

What Gets Deleted

Upon your request, we will delete:

  • Your account information
  • Personal data associated with your account
  • Any usage history and logs linked to your account
